About Cricket

A little bit about me

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field, at the centre of which is a 22-yard (20-metre; 66-foot) pitch with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps. Two players from the batting team stand in front of either wicket holding bats, while one player from the fielding team bowls toward the striker's wicket.

The striker's goal is to hit the bowled ball and switch places with the nonstriker, scoring runs. The fielding team aims to prevent runs by dismissing batters. Cricket shares similarities with baseball and became England's national sport in the 18th century.
